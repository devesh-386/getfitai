FitAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

FitAI ("the app") is a personal fitness app. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data the app stores

All your data — weight, body measurements, body-fat %, workouts, meals, water, sleep, and your API key — is stored locally on your device in a private database. FitAI does not have its own servers and does not upload or sell your data.

Health Connect data

If you connect Health Connect, FitAI reads steps, sleep, active calories, heart rate, exercise, and distance only to display them inside the app. This data stays on your device. FitAI never shares Health Connect data with any third party and does not use it for advertising.

Camera

The food-scan and gym-scan features use your camera to capture a photo you choose to take. That image is sent to the Google Gemini API for analysis (see below) and is not stored by FitAI after analysis.

Google Gemini API

FitAI's AI features use the Google Gemini API with your own API key that you provide. Workout prompts and the photos you scan are sent to Google to generate results. Your use of Gemini is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. FitAI does not see or store this data on any server.

Data deletion

Because all data is on your device, uninstalling the app deletes all of it. You can also clear individual entries within the app.

Children

FitAI is not directed at children under 13.
